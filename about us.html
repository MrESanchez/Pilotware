ABOUT US


Who we are

We are an organization who supplies drones and drone-related software to various users for various purposes. This is possible thanks to our team of highly dedicated software engineers.




Our Vision

Our goal is to ease drones into a vast range of different industries. There are many tasks that people would rather not do due to todays career-driven society. And also many task are plain dangerous and unecessarily difficult when a person does it. With Pilotware we can make workplaces safer whilst promoting steady growth of economy and individuals.





Our Values



Resourcefulness

We wish for our clients to use and abuse our product in as many ways as possible. As long as the drones are used responsibly and ethically. We wish for our products to repeatedly solve problems. Different types of problems may arise in their respective industry. The resourcefulness of our product is what would allow industries to have their problems solved in a fast and effective way.


Sustainability

Our clients are satisfied with the financial decision to invest in our products. Our products must be able to run for long time periods, with very minimal human interaction and with reasonable amount of energy input. We also expect our products to have zero effect on the environment. That may be by reducing noise pollution from drones and reducing any cyber-waste that drones may cause.


Simplicity

We design our product to be very simple to use and to be hassle free. This is done by implementing AI that can make decisions by itself and it can detect any sort of cyber attacks like packet sniffing. We also make our products easy to use by making it customizable to any task, with minimal knowledge in coding needed. Your drone will do what you want thanks to Pilotware.
